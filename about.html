Our Vision & Mission

At AKG STUDY GROUP, we are dedicated to empowering millions of learners across India with practical and easy-to-understand English learning resources. Our mission is to break language barriers and build confidence through daily practice, engaging content, and a supportive community.

Why AKG STUDY GROUP?

We focus on real-world usage rather than complicated grammar rules, making English learning accessible to beginners and intermediate learners alike. Our free courses, downloadable eBooks, and YouTube series are crafted with love and continuous feedback from our growing learner family.

Our Journey

Started as a small initiative in 2023 by language enthusiast Ankit Gupta, AKG STUDY GROUP has grown into a trusted platform with thousands of active learners, multiple eBooks, and a strong social media presence. We continually expand to include new courses, community contests, and collaborations.
